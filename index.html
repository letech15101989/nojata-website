Company Overview

We are an innovative company focused on analyzing and automating processes. Our mission emphasizes tailoring data solutions to meet client-specific needs.

Nojata is an innovative company that specializes in analyzing, collecting data, automating and enhancing work processes with the help of various applications. In future, we take advantage of the opportunities offered by artificial intelligence and robotics in various areas.

Our vision is to create comprehensive documentation for our customers and use the data collected in transparency and effectively. Our goal is to offer solutions that enable the creation and optimization of processes and ensure that the business is based on solid and as accurate as possible information.
